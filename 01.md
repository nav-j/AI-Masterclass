
### Napkin AI

**What it is:** An AI-powered visual-thinking tool.<br>
**Main use:** Turns your ideas or notes into diagrams, mind maps, flowcharts, and visual explanations.<br>
**Best for:** Brainstorming, planning, explaining concepts visually.

### Gamma

**What it is:** An AI presentation and document creator.<br>
**Main use:** Automatically creates slides, documents, and webpages from a short prompt.<br>
**Best for:** Making presentations quickly without design skills.
