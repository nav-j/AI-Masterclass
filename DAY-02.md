## Simple Explanation (Beginner-Friendly)

### Coding
- You write **everything yourself**
- Maximum control and performance
- Requires **time and strong technical skills**
- Examples: Web apps, startups, custom portals

### WordPress
- Uses **ready-made themes and plugins**
- Easy content management

- Advanced features may require paid plugins

> *‚ÄúWordPress balances power and ease.‚Äù*

### AI Tools
